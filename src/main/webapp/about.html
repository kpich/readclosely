ReadClosely :: About
If you want to understand a text, you have to understand every line or sentence of that text. This site is an attempt to help you do just that: comment on (and read what others have written about) history's great works of verse and prose.
Humanity's collective literary intelligence certainly needs some synoptic repository, you know?
